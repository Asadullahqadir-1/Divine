Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which service is right for me?
The best first step is a free discovery call. Divine will listen to your situation and goals and recommend the pathway that makes the most sense for your season and objectives. There's no pressure — just an honest conversation.
Do you work with clients outside Africa?
Yes. While Divine is based in Africa and speaks extensively across the continent, he works with coaching clients and delivers keynotes globally via virtual formats. International in-person engagements are considered on a case-by-case basis.
What results can I expect from coaching?
Clients consistently report greater clarity on their leadership identity and goals, stronger communication and influence, improved team performance, better decision-making, and measurable career or business advancement. Results depend on your commitment to the process.
How far in advance should I book a keynote?
For major conferences and summit events, we recommend booking 2–3 months in advance. For smaller corporate events and workshops, 4–6 weeks is usually sufficient. Contact us as early as possible to secure your preferred date.
Is coaching available in French?
Divine conducts all coaching and speaking engagements in English. For French-language engagements, please reach out to discuss options and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
